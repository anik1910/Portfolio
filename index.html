EvenBoo

My design focused on creating a smooth and structured experience for event booking. I analyzed common user challenges like seat selection, payment, and profile management, then built an intuitive flow that makes the process faster and easier. The interface uses a clean layout with strong visual hierarchy, where bold highlights guide users toward key actions such as booking and checkout.

Web Design User Research Prototyping
02

MazeRunner

I approached design from an entertainment perspective, emphasizing gameplay engagement and fun. I balanced the mechanics by introducing gradually increasing challenges to keep players motivated without overwhelming them. Bright visuals, smooth animations, and dynamic interactions were incorporated to ensure a playful yet immersive experience for users.

Game Design Animation Prototyping
03

Ryoko

I designed a modern travel platform centered on accessibility and trust. The navigation flow was structured so that users could explore destinations, view packages, and make bookings with minimal effort. A fresh and vibrant design style was applied, enhanced with travel-related imagery, to inspire a sense of adventure and encourage users to plan their trips seamlessly.

Travel User Experience Prototyping
